Barcelona Counsillors' Travel Expenses

May 9, 2022 · 26 min read · Open Data BCN plotly treemap  ·
Share on:

Introduction

Owing to the Open Data BCN project, Barcelona City Council provides several datasets which are almost ready for consumption with minimal pre-processing. Among them, the tables of travel expenses of members of the town council (excluding the mayor) by year contain information about cost, duration and destination of the travels of said members since 2019. 1

The information is organized in csv files with a record for each travel accounting id (Numero_de registre in the files) and expense category: travel proper (Desplaçament), lodging (Allotjament) and other (Altres despeses). Each record contains the name of the member, city and country of destination, amount in euros and dates of departure and arrival.
